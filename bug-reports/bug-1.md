# Bug 1: Negative Salary Accepted

## Title
Salary field accepts negative value

## Severity
High

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Login to HRMS
2. Open payroll section
3. Enter salary as -5000
4. Click save

## Expected Result
System should reject negative salary and show validation error.

## Actual Result
System accepts negative salary.

## Impact
Incorrect payroll data may be generated.
