# Bug 3: Attendance Not Reflected in Payroll

## Title
Attendance changes are not updated in salary calculation

## Severity
High

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Login to HRMS
2. Update employee attendance
3. Generate payroll
4. Check salary result

## Expected Result
Updated attendance should affect payroll calculation correctly.

## Actual Result
Payroll shows old attendance data.

## Impact
Employee salary may be calculated incorrectly.
